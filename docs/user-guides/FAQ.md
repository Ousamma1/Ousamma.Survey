# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

**Q: What is the Ousamma Survey System?**
A: A comprehensive survey platform with AI-powered creation, real-time analytics, multi-language support, and advanced features like geolocation tracking and conditional logic.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+. Mobile browsers fully supported.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3), secure authentication (JWT), and comply with GDPR and data protection regulations.

**Q: Can I use the system offline?**
A: Yes. Surveyors can work offline and sync when connected. Survey creators require internet.

## Account & Access

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter email, follow reset link sent to your inbox.

**Q: Can I have multiple users?**
A: Yes. Administrators can create unlimited users with different roles and permissions.

**Q: What are the different user roles?**
A: Admin, Manager, Surveyor, and Analyst - each with specific permissions.

**Q: How do I enable 2FA?**
A: Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication > Enable. Scan QR code with authenticator app.

## Survey Creation

**Q: How many questions can I include?**
A: Unlimited, but we recommend 10-20 for optimal completion rates (3-5 minutes).

**Q: Can I use AI to create surveys?**
A: Yes! Describe your survey and AI generates questions, response options, and optimal flow.

**Q: Which question types are supported?**
A: Text, email, number, date, rating, multiple choice, checkboxes, dropdown, matrix, file upload, geolocation, signature, and more.

**Q: Can I add skip logic?**
A: Yes. Create conditional rules to show/hide questions based on previous answers.

**Q: How do I translate my survey?**
A: Use the multi-language editor to add translations for each question and option.

**Q: Can I preview before publishing?**
A: Yes. Use Preview mode to test all features before making survey active.

## Response Collection

**Q: How many responses can I collect?**
A: Unlimited responses. System scales automatically.

**Q: Can respondents save and continue later?**
A: Yes, if "Allow partial responses" is enabled in survey settings.

**Q: Is anonymous submission allowed?**
A: Yes, configurable per survey.

**Q: How do I prevent duplicate responses?**
A: Enable "One response per device" or "Require login" in survey settings.

**Q: Can I collect location data?**
A: Yes. Add geolocation question type or enable automatic location capture.

## Analytics & Reporting

**Q: Are analytics real-time?**
A: Yes. Dashboard updates within seconds of response submission.

**Q: Can I export data?**
A: Yes. Export to PDF, Excel, CSV, or PowerPoint.

**Q: What analysis features are available?**
A: Response statistics, charts, sentiment analysis, correlation analysis, trend detection, segmentation, and cross-tabulation.

**Q: Can I schedule automated reports?**
A: Yes. Configure in Analytics > Reports > Schedule.

**Q: How long is data retained?**
A: Indefinitely by default. Configure retention policy in Settings > Data Retention.

## Distribution

**Q: How do I share my survey?**
A: Public link, QR code, email, website embed, or mobile app.

**Q: Can I email surveys to respondents?**
A: Yes. Import email list and send via built-in email system.

**Q: How do I generate a QR code?**
A: Automatic. Click "QR Code" button in survey distribution section.

**Q: Can I embed survey on my website?**
A: Yes. Copy embed code and paste into your website.

## Surveyor Management

**Q: How do I add surveyors?**
A: Admin > Surveyors > Add Surveyor. Or bulk import via CSV.

**Q: Can I assign quotas?**
A: Yes. Set target response count and deadline for each surveyor.

**Q: How do I track surveyor performance?**
A: View in Surveyor Dashboard: responses collected, completion rate, time per survey.

**Q: Can surveyors work offline?**
A: Yes. Responses sync automatically when connected.

## Technical Questions

**Q: What are system requirements?**
A:
- Development: 4 CPU cores, 8 GB RAM, 20 GB storage
- Production: 8+ CPU cores, 16 GB RAM, 100 GB SSD

**Q: Can I self-host?**
A: Yes. Docker deployment available for on-premise installation.

**Q: Is there an API?**
A: Yes. Full REST API with OpenAPI documentation. See `/docs/api/`

**Q: Which databases are used?**
A: MongoDB (primary), Redis (cache), Kafka (events).

**Q: Can I integrate with other systems?**
A: Yes. Use REST API, webhooks, or Zapier integration.
